title: Email response error messages
date: 2006-04-04

I hate spam as much as anyone. What I hate more are the spam
gateways that prevent people from connecting without reading
some verbose server error messages, or requiring that you
send mail to allow@domain. Here is a more specific error
message I got back from a server. It was in response to a
followup on a newsgroup posting:

         The following addresses had permanent fatal errors

         (reason: 550 5.7.0 Fuck off)

         Transcript of session follows
         while talking to ..:
         >>> >>> DATA
         <<< 550 5.7.0 Fuck off
         554 5.0.0 Service unavailable
